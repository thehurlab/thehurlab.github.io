Our Research
🔬

CRISPR Diagnostics

Our flagship MUTE-Seq platform harnesses Cas protein activity to achieve unprecedented 0.005% detection sensitivity — enabling identification of rare mutations even in complex clinical samples at trace concentrations.

MUTE-Seq
🧬

Gene & Cell Therapy

We engineer precise therapeutic strategies using advanced CRISPR machinery. Our pipeline spans target identification, Cas optimization, and translational delivery systems aimed at correcting the root causes of inherited disease.

Active
🔀

RNA Biology

We map the epitranscriptome and decode RNA–protein interaction networks using CRISPR-based functional screens. Our discoveries reveal how RNA regulation drives gene expression, and how dysregulation contributes to cancer.
