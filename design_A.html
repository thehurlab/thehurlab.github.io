Our Research

CRISPR Diagnostics

Development of MUTE-Seq, a groundbreaking CRISPR-based diagnostic platform achieving 0.005% detection sensitivity — enabling identification of rare mutations in complex biological samples.

Gene & Cell Therapy

Engineering next-generation therapeutic strategies using CRISPR genome editing tools. Our work targets inherited diseases with precise, safe, and effective gene correction approaches.

RNA Biology

Investigating the complex regulatory landscape of RNA molecules. We study RNA–protein interactions, epitranscriptomic modifications, and their roles in disease and cellular function.
