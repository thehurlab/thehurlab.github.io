What We Do

Research Areas

Our lab pioneers CRISPR-based tools for diagnostics, therapeutic gene editing, and RNA biology.

🧬

CRISPR Diagnostics

Ultra-sensitive nucleic acid detection using MUTE-Seq technology for clinical applications.

Hover to learn more →
MUTE-Seq

CRISPR Diagnostics

We developed MUTE-Seq, achieving 0.005% detection sensitivity — enabling early disease detection at near-single-molecule resolution.

  • Liquid biopsy for cancer
  • Infectious disease diagnostics
  • Point-of-care platforms
💊

Gene & Cell Therapy

Precision genome engineering for therapeutic correction of genetic disorders.

Hover to learn more →
Therapeutics

Gene & Cell Therapy

Harnessing CRISPR-Cas systems for safe, efficient therapeutic editing in primary cells and model organisms.

  • HDR-based correction strategies
  • CAR-T cell engineering
  • In vivo delivery systems
🔬

RNA Biology

Decoding RNA regulatory networks and developing RNA-targeting CRISPR tools.

Hover to learn more →
RNA Science

RNA Biology

Investigating non-coding RNA functions and engineering Cas13-based tools for transcriptome editing.

  • A-to-I RNA editing (ADARs)
  • Cas13 therapeutic tools
  • RNA structure-function analysis
