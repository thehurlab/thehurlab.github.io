Research Areas

Active Programs

CRISPR Diagnostics (MUTE-Seq)

Our flagship platform achieves 0.005% detection sensitivity for rare mutations. MUTE-Seq leverages Cas proteins as molecular scissors to enrich and identify low-abundance variants in clinical settings.

Learn more →

Gene & Cell Therapy

We design precision genome editing strategies for inherited diseases, combining advanced Cas variants with optimized delivery systems to achieve safe, efficient therapeutic corrections in patient cells.

Learn more →

RNA Biology

We dissect epitranscriptomic regulation and RNA–protein interaction networks. Our work reveals how RNA modifications orchestrate gene expression programs, with implications for cancer and developmental disorders.
